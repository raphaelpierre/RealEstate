Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our app

What platforms is the RealEstate app available on?

Currently, the RealEstate app is available for iOS devices running iOS 15.0 or later. We're working on an Android version that will be released in the near future.

How do I list my property on the app?

To list your property, create an account and navigate to the "My Properties" section. Click on "Add New Property" and follow the step-by-step guide to enter your property details, upload photos, and set your price.

Can I use the app in languages other than English?

Yes, the app currently supports English and French. We're actively working on adding more languages to make the app accessible to users worldwide.

What's the difference between the free and premium versions?

The free version allows you to browse properties, use basic search filters, save up to 5 favorites, and contact property owners. The premium version removes ads, provides advanced search filters, unlimited favorites, featured placement in search results, an analytics dashboard, and priority customer support.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your App Store account settings. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions, find the RealEstate app subscription, and tap "Cancel Subscription."
